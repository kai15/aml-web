ABOUT US

PT. Agung Makmur Logistik has been established as a freight forwarding, customs broker and supply chain logistics in indonesia since 2014 and we are now growing with 3 branches and representative offices in Jakarta, Surabaya & Semarang. We have the competence and qualifications in the field of handling customs services, cargo and transportation management services.
